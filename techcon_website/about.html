History

Tech-Con Systems, Inc. is located in Slidell, Louisiana just outside of New Orleans and has been in business since 1985. We have professional engineers on staff to provide structural designs and to give our customers technical assistance. We utilize the best computer hardware and software available to facilitate and expedite slab designs. Additionally, we prepare and show drawings prepared using a CAD system.

Mission

Our goal is to provide the highest quality customer service and to produce a superior product.
